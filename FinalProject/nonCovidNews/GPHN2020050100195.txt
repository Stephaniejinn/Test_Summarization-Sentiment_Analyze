Title : Two die in Tampico, one in Victoria for covid-19
Ariticle : 
Tamaulipas exceeds 500 cases of covid-19, 34 new positive cases were confirmed to the SARS-COV2 virus and the death of 3 patients, with which it accumulates 516 confirmed, 63 recovered and 23 deaths.
Gloria Molina Gamboa, secretary of Health reported that these are 8 patients from Ciudad Victoria and are a 28-year-old woman, a 34-year-old man, a 53-year-old woman, a 37-year-old man, a 39-year-old man, a 48-year-old man, a 22-year-old man, and a 39-year-old man.
From Matamoros, a 44-year-old woman, a 54-year-old man, a 35-year-old man, 2 underage, a 39-year-old woman, a 34-year-old woman and a 56-year-old man.
