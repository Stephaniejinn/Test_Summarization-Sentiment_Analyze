Title : Health: Vietnam Records 23.5K Dengue Fever, 4,579 HFM Cases in Jan-Apr: GSO
Ariticle : 
Vietnam recorded 23,500 dengue fever cases, including four deaths, and 4,579 hand-foot-mouth (HFM) cases in the first four months of this year, according to the latest figures from the governmentâ€™s General Statistics Office (GSO).
In the period, the country recorded 177 measles, 118 viral encephalitis cases, and 419 food poisoning cases (including 12 deaths).
As of April 18, Vietnam had 210,400 people infected with HIV, including 97,137 people entering AIDS stage, while around 98,655 people had been killed by HIV/AIDS.
Notably, Vietnam has recorded 270 cases infected with the coronavirus disease (COVID-19) as of 6:00 a.m. on April 27, 225 of them were discharged from hospital, the GSO said.
