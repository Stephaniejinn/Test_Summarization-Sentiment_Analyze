Title : 16 days without Covid-19, the latest suspected case of dengue fever
Ariticle : 

According to the Ministry of Health, from 6pm on 16/4 (the date Vietnam announced the Covid-19th case of the 268 th), to 18:00 on 2/5, Vietnam spent 116 days without new cases due to transmission. infection in the community.


Up to now, the total number of people in close contact and entry from the epidemic area are being monitored for health (isolation): 30,517, of which:

- Centralized isolation at the hospital: 244

- Isolation concentrated in other establishments: 5,540

- Isolation at home and place of residence: 24,733

Regarding treatment, there are now 219/270 patients recovered. Among cases being treated at health facilities, there have been 21 negative cases from 1-2 times with SARS-CoV-2.

The case of male TN K patient, born in 1982 (hometown: Hoang Mai, Nghe An) who are living in Gia Lam, Hanoi, has high fever for many days, causing public concern about SARS-CoV-2 virus infection.

The patient did not have a history of contact with a suspect covid-19 person. From April 22, there was continuous fever, on April 26, Duc Giang Hospital patient was tested for dengue IgM (+) test.

On April 29, the patient had a higher fever, increased dyspnea, was transferred to Hanoi Heart Hospital. Here, do quick test test Covid-19 (+).

With the positive rapid test, the patient was transferred to the Central Tropical Hospital from 23:30 on May 1 in a state of shortness of breath, exhausted,. The RT-PCR test gave a negative result with Covid 19. The patient was resuscitated, oxygenated, treated in the direction of dengue superinfection.
