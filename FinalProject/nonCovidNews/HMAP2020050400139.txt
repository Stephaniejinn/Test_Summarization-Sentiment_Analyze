Title : PRO/AH/EDR> Yellow fever - Americas (07): Brazil (SC) -
Ariticle : 
Santa Catarina has 15 patients infected with yellow fever, according to information issued by the office of Epidemiological Surveillance this past Monday (27 Apr 2020).There are 2 cases more in relation to the previous bulletin issued 15 days earlier. Of the total number of people who have contracted the disease, 6 were diagnosed in Blumenau, in the Vale of Itajai.The number of deaths remains the same; 2 deaths in 2020, both registered in the month of March 2020. The 1st death is a man
