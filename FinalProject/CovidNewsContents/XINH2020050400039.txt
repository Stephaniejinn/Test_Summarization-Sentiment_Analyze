Title : A post-90s couple's shared battle in the laboratory against COVID-19 epidemic
Ariticle : 
Ji Xiaolei (L) helps his wife Guo Chen wear protective suit in a laboratory of Nantong center for disease control and prevention in Nantong, east China's Jiangsu Province, April 26, 2020. (Photo by Xu Congjun/Xinhua)
A young couple fight hand in hand in their shared "battlefield" against the COVID-19 epidemic in a laboratory in east China's Jiangsu.
NANTONG, May 4 (Xinhua) -- Working at a detection team of the microbiology laboratory in the Nantong center for disease control and prevention, Ji Xiaolei and his wife Guo Chen, both were born in the 90s, have worked hard with their colleagues to conduct novel coronavirus detections since the COVID-19 epidemic outbreak, with a highest daily detection samples reaching 550.
Facing the sudden outbreak of the novel coronavirus disease, young Chinese of all ethnic groups have actively participated in the all-out people's war against the epidemic.
