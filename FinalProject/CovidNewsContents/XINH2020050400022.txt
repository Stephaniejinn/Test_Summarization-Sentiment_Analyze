Title : Asia Album: Bangladesh expands COVID-19 testing
Ariticle : 
A medical worker collects a sample for COVID-19 testing at a hospital in Dhaka, Bangladesh, on May 3, 2020. (Str/Xinhua)
DHAKA, May 4 (Xinhua) -- Bangladesh has expanded its COVID-19 testing as 665 new COVID-19 cases were reported Sunday, the biggest daily jump in new cases since March 8 when confirmed cases were first reported in the country.
"The total number of positive cases is 9,455 and death toll stands currently at 177," a senior health ministry official, Professor Nasima Sultana, said during a briefing on Sunday afternoon.
According to the official, 5,368 samples were tested in the last 24 hours across Bangladesh.
