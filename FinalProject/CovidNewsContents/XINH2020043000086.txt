Title : China hands over medical aid to South Africa
Ariticle : 


South Africa's Western Cape on Tuesday received badly-needed medical supplies from China amid the rapid spread of COVID-19 in the province, the epicenter of the virus in the country.Albert Fritz, minister for Community Safety for the Western Cape, received the supplies at a ceremony held at the Chinese Consulate in Cape Town.The supplies include masks, gloves, hand sanitizers and other materials.Fritz thanked the Chinese Consulate and the Chinese local community for making this happen. He said Western Cape is badly in need of medical supplies as the province is griped by the pandemic.
