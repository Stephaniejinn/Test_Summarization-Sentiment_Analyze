The main epidemiologist of Ministry of health reported that the coronavirus can affect any organs
By
WhatsApp
Studies of the manifestations of coronavirus indicate that it can affect any organs and systems of man, said the chief epidemiologist of the Ministry of health of the Russian Federation Nikolay Briko. According to him, the accumulated data in the U.S. and Western Europe say that from the coronavirus killed twice as many men than women.
“Among hospitalized patients more than 90% are pneumonia, DIC (disseminated intravascular coagulation.— “B”) — more than 50%, acute kidney injury is about 15% and myocardial damage more than 20%”, — he told in interview “to the parliamentary newspaper”.
