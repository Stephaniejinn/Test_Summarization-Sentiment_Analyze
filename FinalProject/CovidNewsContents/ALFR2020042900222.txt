Reallocated cases
Two COVID-19 cases which were previously allocated to Klerksdorp have been reallocated to Potchefstroom.
Recovieries
Sixteen COVID-19 confirmed positive persons have recovered. Thirteen recovered persons are in Bojanala District, two in Dr. Kenneth Kaunda District and one in Ngaka Modiri Molema District.
