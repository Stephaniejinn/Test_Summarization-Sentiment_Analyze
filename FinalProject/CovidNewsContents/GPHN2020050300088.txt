Title : Coronavirus Latest Updates: 39,980 COVID-19 cases in India; Army, IAF, Navy pay tribute to frontline workers
Ariticle : 
Coronavirus India Updates (State-Wise), Coronavirus Lockdown LIVE Updates: The total number of COVID-19 cases in India is inching towards the 40,000-mark.Data provided by the Union Health Ministry showed that at least 1,301 people have died due to COVID-19 so far across the country, including 83 deaths in the last 24 hours. As per the update on Sunday morning, the total number of confirmed COVID-19 cases stood at 39,980, after a record jump of 2,411 cases on Saturday. A total of 10,633people have recovered. Nearly 10 lakh tests have been conducted so far across India, the ministry said.
On Friday, the Ministry of Home Affairs extended the lockdown by two weeks from May 4, with a new set of guidelines to be functional for different zones and hotspots.
