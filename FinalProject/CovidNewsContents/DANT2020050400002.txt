Title : 4/5 morning: no recorded cases of Covid-19 community
Ariticle : 

So far, Vietnam has recorded 271 cases of Covid-19, of which 219 patients were cured and discharged. There are still 51 patients treated at health facilities, 21 cases were negative 1-2 times with SARS-CoV-2.

 




The remaining patients have relatively stable health status. However, the case of the British pilot in Ho Chi Minh City is very worrying, when health progresses worse, pneumothorax.

Currently, there are 27,409 quarantined in the country, in which:

- Isolator concentration in hospital: 238

- Isolation concentrated in other establishments: 5,871

- Isolation at home and place of residence: 21,300

Ministry of Health said, since 268 cases of young girls in Ha Giang was announced on the morning of 16/4, so far no new cases have been detected in the community. 3 new cases were discovered later, the latest is that 271 was announced on the afternoon of 3/5, all cases were intrusive, isolated immediately upon entry, so there is no possibility of spreading in the community.

The absence of new cases for 18 days is a good sign in the prevention of epidemics. However, the world epidemic is still very complicated, there are cases without symptoms, so the Ministry of Health continues to advise people not to be subjective, to practice personal hygiene such as washing hand soap. room often, wear a mask when going out ... in case of risk.
