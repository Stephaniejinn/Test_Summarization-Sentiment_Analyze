Title : Angola: Portugal/COVID-19 - 12 More Angolans Infected in Lisbon
Ariticle : 
Lisbon â€” Twelve more Angolan nationals have tested positive for covid-19 in Lisbon, Portugal, bringing to 25 the number of Angolans infected, eight of whom travelled to that country for medical treatment.
According to the local health authorities, so far two have recovered and one died.
13 of those infected are interned in various hospitals and 12 are recovering at home, under medical surveillance of local authorities and assistance by the Health Services of the Embassy of Angola in Lisbon.
Those recovered are a non-resident couple who have been discharged from hospital.
There are nearly three million people infected with the new coronavirus around the world since the first positive case was detected in December last year in China.
