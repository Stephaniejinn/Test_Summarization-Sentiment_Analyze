Title : Just in: Russia registers new single-day record of 10,633 new COVID-19 cases
Ariticle : 
Utility service vehicles disinfect a road in Moscow, Russia, on April 24, 2020. (Xinhua/Evgeny Sinitsyn)
MOSCOW, May 3 (Xinhua) -- Russia on Sunday reported a new single-day record with 10,633 new COVID-19 cases confirmed over the last 24 hours, raising its total number of infections to 134,687, its coronavirus response center said in a statement.
