Title : Coronavirus live updates: DHS report says China hid outbreak's severity; British PM Boris Johnson shares details of hospitalization
Ariticle : 
China concealed the severity of its COVID-19 outbreak and how contagious the coronavirus was to buy time in early January to acquire medical supplies to fight it, according to a new report from the Department of Homeland Security.
