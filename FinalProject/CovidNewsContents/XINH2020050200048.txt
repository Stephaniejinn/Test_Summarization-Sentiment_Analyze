Title : How Italy's Chinese community unscathed by coronavirus
Ariticle : 


Zero COVID-19 infection has been reported in the Chinese community in Prato, one of Italy's largest Chinese communities.Mayor of Prato Matteo Biffoni and city councilor Macro Wong tell how the community is unscathed by the coronavirus and helps the city fight the virus.
