Title : Six more fall prey to virus
Ariticle : 
Six more people died of coronavirus while 396 others tested positive for the virus during the last 24 hours across the province taking total deaths to 106 and confirmed cases to 6,220 in Punjab.
Of those who died of the virus, four succumbed to the disease in Lahore while two in Gujranwala.
The situation is alarming in the provincial capital which reported 296 out of total 396 confirmed cases besides most deaths.
However, no new confirmed case was reported from the quarantine centres where the pilgrims arrived from Iran and the Tableeghi Jamaat members were isolated.
A spokesperson for the health department said total confirmed cases reached 1,765 in Lahore so far.
He said the 27 patients tested positive for the virus were critical all over the province while 1,850 have been discharged so far.
The new confirmed cases were also reported from Faisalabad, Rawalpindi, Sheikhupura, Gujranwala, Pakpattan, Jhang etc.
