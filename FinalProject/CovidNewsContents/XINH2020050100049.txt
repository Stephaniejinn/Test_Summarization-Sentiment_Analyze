Title : Chinese mainland reports 6 new imported COVID-19 cases
Ariticle : 
A staff member of the customs checks the health declaration information of a newly arrived inbound passenger in Chongqing, southwest China, March 29, 2020. 
(Xinhua/Huang Wei)
BEIJING, May 1 (Xinhua) -- The Chinese mainland reported six new imported cases of the novel coronavirus disease (COVID-19) Thursday, bringing the total number of imported cases to 1,670, the National Health Commission said Friday.
Of the total imported cases, 1,165 had been discharged from hospitals after recovery, and 505 were being treated in hospitals with seven in severe conditions, the commission said.
No deaths had been reported from the imported cases.
