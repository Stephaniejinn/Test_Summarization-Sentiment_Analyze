Title : Asia Album: Nepal Overseas Chinese Business Association provides food relief packages to lockdown-affected Nepali families
Ariticle : 
Zhang Jinxiang (R), a Chinese entrepreneur and the representative of Nepal Overseas Chinese Business Association, hands over a sack of rice to a Nepali woman at Bungamati in Lalitpur, Nepal, April 29, 2020. (Xinhua/Sulav Shrestha)
KATHMANDU, April 30 (Xinhua) -- With the support of China Foundation for Poverty Alleviation (CFPA), Nepal Overseas Chinese Business Association provided food relief packages to around 250 poor families who were affected by a nationwide lockdown imposed in Nepal since March 24 to battle the spread of COVID-19.
