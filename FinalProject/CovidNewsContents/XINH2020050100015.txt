Title : Just in: China firmly supports Czech Republic's fight against COVID-19: Xi
Ariticle : 
A Red Cross worker (R) passes face masks to a volunteer, who will deliver them to seniors in need, in Prague, the Czech Republic, April 20, 2020. (Photo by Dana Kesnerova/Xinhua)
BEIJING, April 30 (Xinhua) -- Chinese President Xi Jinping said on Thursday that China firmly supports the Czech Republic's fight against COVID-19.
People unload medical supplies purchased from China at the Prague Airport in Prague, the Czech Republic, March 20, 2020.
 (Xinhua/Yang Xiaohong)
In a telephone conversation with his Czech counterpart, Milos Zeman, Xi also said that the Chinese side is ready to share information and experience concerning COVID-19 containment with the Czech Republic via bilateral and the China-Central and Eastern European countries (CEECs) cooperation channels.
