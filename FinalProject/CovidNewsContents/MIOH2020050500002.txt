Title : Brazil recorded 105 222 cases of coronavirus and 7,288 deaths from the disease
Ariticle : 
The information was updated until 16:30 on Monday (4) and passed by the State Health Departments across the country
The Ministry of Health registered 105,222 cases of coronavirus and 7,288 deaths from the disease in Brazil until 16:30 on Monday (4). The information was updated and passed by the State Health Departments across the country.
In the last 24 hours were 4,075 new cases and 263 new deaths, and most of it is related to other periods, but was registered yesterday to today.
Although many municipalities in the country still do not register cases of the disease in general, the coronavirus is present in all states of the country. Currently, SÃ£o Paulo concentrates most of the reports, with 32,187 cases and 2,654 deaths. Rio de Janeiro is in second place with 11,721 cases and 1,065 deaths. The state that registers less notification is Tocantins confirmations 267 cases and six deaths. Currently, the fatality rate of the disease is 6.9%.
