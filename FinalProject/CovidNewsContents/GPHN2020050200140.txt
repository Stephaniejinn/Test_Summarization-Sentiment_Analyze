Title : coronavirus pandemic local, state updates; NW Louisiana COVID-19 cases nearing 2,500
Ariticle : 
The number of confirmed positive COVID-19 cases in Louisiana's Region 7 is approaching 2,500.
Caddo Parish has the most positive cases in the nine parishes in Region 7. Caddo Parish saw an increase of 39 cases to 1,579 cases Friday. The parish has 120 deaths related to the virus, according to the Caddo Parish Coroner's Office.
Bossier Parish has 287 cases after an increase of one case Friday. There have been 17 deaths related to COVID-19 in the parish.
DeSoto Parish is at 194 cases after an increase of four cases Friday. There have been 11 COVID-19-related deaths in the parish.
Webster Parish saw an increase of four cases to 92 positive cases Friday. There have been four deaths due to the virus in the parish.
Natchitoches Parish is up five cases to 84 total. The parish remained at four COVID-19-related deaths.
Bienville Parish has three new cases giving the parish 77 total with 13 deaths related to the virus.
Claiborne Parish has 59 total cases after having five new cases Friday. Claiborne Parish has seven deaths due to COVID-19.
Red River Parish saw an increase of one case to 25. There have been five deaths related to the virus in the parish.
Sabine Parish had a new case to raise its total to 17. There has been one death in the parish related to COVID-19.
Outside if Region 7, Lincoln Parish reported two new cases and one additional death due to the virus. Lincoln Parish has 85 cases and eight deaths.
