Title : Youth Day: portraits of young construction workers in SW China

Many young workers have stuck to their positions on the National Youth Day. 
ZUNYI, May 4 (Xinhua) -- 
National Youth Day is celebrated in China every year on May 4, a time to reflect on the heroic roles young Chinese played in history.
Facing the sudden outbreak of the novel coronavirus disease, young Chinese of all ethnic groups have actively participated in the all-out people's war against the epidemic.
Youngsters are called on to pass on the May Fourth spirit and to work hard and forge ahead with their fellow Chinese along the new long march to realizing the Chinese Dream of national rejuvenation.
