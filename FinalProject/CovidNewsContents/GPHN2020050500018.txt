Title : New US Report Accuses China Of Intentionally Hiding COVID-19 Outbreak Reality To Hoard Masks, Supplies
Ariticle : 
A new report released by the United States Department of Homeland Security (DHS) officials has accused China of hidings facts related to the severity of coronavirus outbreak in Wuhan when it was in its earliest stages.
