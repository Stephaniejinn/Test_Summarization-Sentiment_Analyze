Title : Just in: Tajikistan reports first 15 COVID-19 cases -- Health Ministry
Ariticle : 
DUSHANBE, April 30 (Xinhua) -- Tajikistan has registered the first 15 coronavirus cases, the country's Health Ministry said on Thursday.
According to the ministry, the 15 cases have been confirmed respectively in the capital of Dushanbe and the northern province of Sughd.
