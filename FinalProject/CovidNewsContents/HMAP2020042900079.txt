Title : PRO/AH/EDR> COVID-19 update (138): India, wild tiger, fatal -
Ariticle : 
[edited]All forest department officials of Pench Tiger Reserve, who were quarantined after the death of T-21 tiger, have completed their quarantine period and all are fit now. The tiger died on [4 Apr 2020] in Pench Tiger Reserve. National Tiger Conservation Authority (NTCA) had issued alert after tiger was found COVID-19 positive in US. Pench Tiger Reserve is spread in Seoni and Chhindwara districts in Madhya Pradesh.The T-21 tiger died due to lungs infection and high fever.
