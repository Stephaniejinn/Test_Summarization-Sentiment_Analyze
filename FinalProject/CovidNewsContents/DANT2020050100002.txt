Title : 1/5 morning: Vietnam did not have a new case of Covid-19
Ariticle : 

From 18h on 30/4 to 6h on 1/5, Vietnam had 0 new cases of Covid-19.

Total close contacts and entry from epidemic areas are under health monitoring (isolation): 47,735, of which:

- Centralized isolation at the hospital: 272

- Isolation concentrated in other establishments: 12,246

- Isolation at home and place of residence: 35,217


Regarding the treatment situation, according to the report of the Treatment Sub-Committee of the National Steering Committee for Disease Prevention and Control Covid-19:

- Number of negative cases for the first time with SARS-CoV-2: 10 cases.

- Number of negative cases 2 or more with SARS-CoV-2: 5 cases.

By the morning of 1/5, Vietnam recorded 270 cases in 30 provinces and cities; ranked 128 out of 212 countries and territories affected by Covid-19. There were 219 patients announced to be cured; 12 returning positive patients continued to receive hospital treatment. There are still 51 patients being treated at 9 medical facilities. A total of 261,741 samples have been tested so far (positive: 270).

Epidemic in the world is still complicated, increasing the number of cases and deaths. To date, more than 3.2 million cases of Covid-19 have been recorded globally in 212 countries and territories (including 2 cruise ships); 228,505 (7.1%) of deaths.

Countries with more than 100,000 cases: USA, Spain, Italy, France, Germany, UK and Turkey. The current epidemic centers in the world are concentrated in: USA, Europe (Italy, Spain, France, Germany, England). Especially, the number of cases has increased rapidly in the US (more than 1 million cases, 61,669 deaths), then to Spain, Italy, France, England and Germany.

In Southeast Asia, the number of cases continues to rise rapidly in Singapore (16,169 cases, 14 deaths), Indonesia (10,118 cases, 792 deaths), Philippines (8,488 cases, 568 deaths), Malaysia. (6,002 cases, 102 deaths).
