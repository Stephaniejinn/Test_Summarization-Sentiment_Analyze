Title : Brazil recorded 114 715 cases of coronavirus and 7,921 deaths from the disease
Ariticle : 
The information was updated until 19h on Tuesday (5) and passed by the State Health Departments across the country. Of the total confirmed cases, 48,221 have recovered and the other 48,221 are being assisted
The Ministry of Health registered 114,715 cases of coronavirus and 7,921 deaths from the disease in Brazil until 20h on Tuesday (5), according to information have been updated and passed by the State Health Departments across the country. They are considered recovered after contracting the disease 48 221 people, representing 42% of all confirmed cases. Currently, there are monitoring other 58,573 people (51.1%) and 1,579 deaths remain under investigation.
In the last 24 hours were 6,935 new cases and 600 new deaths, and most of it is related to other periods, but was registered yesterday to today. Thus, in the last 24 hours there were, in fact, 76 deaths.
Although many municipalities in the country still do not register cases of the disease in general, the coronavirus is present in all states of the country. Currently, São Paulo concentrates most of the reports, with 34,053 cases and 2,851 deaths. Rio de Janeiro is in second place with 12,391 cases and 1,123 deaths. The state registers fewer notifications is Mato Grosso do Sul, with 283 confirmations of cases and ten deaths. Tocantins now has 303 cases and seven deaths.
