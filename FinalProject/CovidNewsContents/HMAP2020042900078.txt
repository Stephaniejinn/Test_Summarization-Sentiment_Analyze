The Egyptian Ministry of Health on [Tue 28 Apr 2020] confirmed 260 new COVID-19 cases and 22 more deaths from the disease.This has brought the total number of coronavirus cases in the country to 5042, including 359 deaths and the total recoveries to 1304, the ministry's spokesman Khaled Megahed said in a press statement.He added that 30% of the mortalities occurred before arrival to the hospital, and 20% occurred within 48 hours of hospital admission.--Communicated
