Title : Shanghai reports no increase in COVID-19 cases
Ariticle : 
A customer buys products at a time-honored food store in east China's Shanghai Municipality, April 26, 2020. (Xinhua/Wang Xiang)
SHANGHAI, May 2 (Xinhua) -- Shanghai reported no new imported cases of the novel coronavirus disease (COVID-19) and zero increase in locally transmitted COVID-19 case on Friday, the municipal health commission said Saturday.
As of Friday, there were no asymptomatic cases under medical observation in Shanghai.
The commission said a total of 313 imported cases had been reported in Shanghai by Friday. Among them, 32 patients remained in hospital for treatment with two of them in critical condition.
Shanghai had reported 339 locally transmitted confirmed cases by Friday, including seven deaths.
