Title : Latest coronavirus figures for St Helens
Ariticle : 
THE latest number of confirmed coronavirus cases in St Helens is 655, say government figures.
The latest statistics, released on Saturday, May 2 recorded 655 confirmed cases in the borough.
Meanwhile, there are 554 confirmed cases in Knowsley.
There have been 152 people confirmed to have died after they tested positive for COVID-19 at St Helens and Knowsley NHS Trust.
Nationally, as of Saturday's figures there have been 182,260 confirmed cases, with 28,131 people having died after testing positive.
