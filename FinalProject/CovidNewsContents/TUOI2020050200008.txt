Thus, the total number of deaths from COVID-19 in the United States is now nearly 65,000 (64,789 to be exact), while the total number of confirmed cases is more than 1.1 million as of 20:30 on 1-5 US time, 7:30 this morning 2-5 hours in Vietnam.
The United States is still the country with the highest number of people infected and dying from COVID-19.
In related developments, according to Reuters news agency, Mr. Trump seems to have raised the prediction of the number of patients who will die of COVID-19 in the US when at an event in the White House on May 1, saying that he hi fewer than 100,000 will die from the disease.
This is an estimate of Trump&#39;s higher upper limit, compared to the predicted 60,000-70,000 people who could die from COVID-19 as he discussed this Monday (April 27). ).
In March, Dr. Anthony Fauci, the leading infectious disease expert of the US government, said Americans should be prepared to face the situation that 100,000 people will die from COVID-19.
However, regardless of the epidemic situation, there are still hot developments, according to AFP news agency, there have been some more states in the US loosen the measures to blockade the epidemic on 1-5.
Texas became the largest state in the United States to declare easing the blockade despite recording 50 deaths on April 30.
Shops, restaurants, movie theaters, shopping malls, museums and libraries are allowed to reopen in Texas but with only 25% operating capacity. The governor called on people to wear masks and continue to maintain the distance necessary to prevent infection.
Meanwhile, New York state has decided to close schools for the rest of this school year, according to Governor Andrew Cuomo&#39;s announcement on May 1.
The decision was made in the context of the state of New York still very worried that reopening too soon will give rise to a new wave of infection.
Earlier, on April 11, Mayor of New York City, Mr. Bill de Blasio, said students would not be able to return to school this school year because of the epidemic.
