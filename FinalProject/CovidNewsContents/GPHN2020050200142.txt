Title : ECI confirms 1st COVID-19 case on staff
Ariticle : 
Eastern Correctional Institution confirmed its first case of COVID-19, the disease caused by the novel coronavirus.
The case is not an officer or inmate. Data instead reflected the first positive is a non-uniformed member of staff.
As of Thursday evening, cases were confirmed across all 17 of Maryland's correctional facilities reported in the state's Department of Public Safety and Correctional Services COVID-19 Semiweekly Update.
There are 162 officers, 52 inmates and 12 non-uniformed staff testing positive throughout the state. No officers or staff have died, according to the data, but two inmates have died with the virus.
Across this population, 70 people have reportedly recovered from the disease.
No further details on the ECI case were provided.
