Title : Just In: Italy registers 203,591 coronavirus infections, death toll at 27,682
Ariticle : 
A man with a face mask is seen in Rome, Italy, on April 26, 2020. (Photo by Elisa Lingria/ Xinhua)
ROME, April 29 (Xinhua) -- The coronavirus pandemic has claimed 27,682 lives in locked-down Italy, bringing the total number of infections, fatalities and recoveries so far to 203,591, according to the latest data released by the country's Civil Protection Department on Wednesday.
