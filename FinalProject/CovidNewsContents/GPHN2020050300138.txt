Title : Hoshiarpur witnesses rise in number of Corona cases
Ariticle : 
Hoshiarpur, May 3 -- After detection of 74 Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19) cases in past 24 hours, the district witnessed a steep rise of the pandemic with a tally of 86 pandemic cases. In a statement here on Sunday, Civil Surgeon, Hoshiarpur said the district registered 78 active cases of Coronavirus of which 74 were pilgrims who returned from Takht Sri Hazur Sahib, Nanded, Maharashtra. He pointed out that with the Corona detection of Nanded-returnees, the district which was moving towards green zone, had once again fall in red zone. As many as 838 samples of suspected coronavirus patients had been taken in the district of which reports of 632 were found negative. Reports of 120 samples were still awaited. He said that 158 pilgrims had returned from the shrine of Hazur Sahib of which 74 were found positive. One each positive case had been detected in local Mohalla Kamalpur, Hajipur, Possi, and Budhbar villages. One positive case of village Kotli had been admitted in a Ludhiana hospital.
 He said that all the entry points of Mohalla Kamalpur had been sealed. Besides sanitising the entire Mohalla Kamalpur, anti-larva teams had been sent to make a door-to-door survey in this regard. UNI XC JS JW1717
