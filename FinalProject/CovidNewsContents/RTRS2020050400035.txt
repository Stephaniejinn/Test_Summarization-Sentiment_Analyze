Title : Singapore's health ministry confirms 573 new coronavirus cases
Ariticle : 
SINGAPORE (Reuters) - Singapore’s health ministry said on Monday it confirmed 573 new coronavirus cases, taking the city-state’s tally of infections to 18,778.
