Title : English hospital COVID-19 death toll rises 445 to 19,746
Ariticle : 
LONDON (Reuters) - A further 445 people who tested positive for COVID-19 have died in English hospitals, bringing the total there to 19,746, the health service said.  
Of the 445, 27 had no known underlying health condition and were aged between 14 and 94.
