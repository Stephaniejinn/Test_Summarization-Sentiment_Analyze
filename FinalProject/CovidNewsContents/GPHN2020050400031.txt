Title : Al Ansari Exchange Supports the Humanitarian Efforts of Emirates Red Crescent With Million Dirham Donation
Ariticle : 
Al Ansari Exchange, the UAE-based foreign exchange and worldwide money transfer company, has donated AED 1million to Emirates Red Crescent, as part of the organization’s charitable efforts to combat the coronavirus outbreak. The donation will go towards the Fund of the UAE: Homeland of Humanity Campaign.
Mohammed Al Ansari, Chairman of Al Ansari Exchange, said, “Al Ansari Exchange has agreed to donate AED 1 million to Emirates Red Crescent to support their relief efforts in tackling the Covid-19 situation. The donation will go towards the relief work including supporting the distribution of medical supplies needed as a result of the Covid-19 pandemic, providing assistance to orphans, people of determination, the elderly and those with chronic diseases and ensuring the most vulnerable families and those with low incomes are protected.”
Al Ansari Exchange has launched a series of charitable and social initiatives and programs in vital sectors such as education, health and others. The company is also active in supporting relief to governments in various regions around the globe.
