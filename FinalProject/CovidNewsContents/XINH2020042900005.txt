Title : Renovation of Notre-Dame Cathedral in Paris resumes
Ariticle : 
PARIS, April 28 (Xinhua) -- The renovation of Notre-Dame Cathedral in Paris has resumed after a suspension since March 17 due to the COVID-19 epidemic, the public agency overseeing the project announced.
