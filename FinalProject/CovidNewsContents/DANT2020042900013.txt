Title : The British pilot turned negative for Covid-19 but was heavy
Ariticle : 

Information from the Department of Health, Ho Chi Minh City on April 29 said that there were 4 cases of Covid-19 in the city. Currently, 3 cases of re-positive are being cared for and treated at Cu Chi field hospital and 1 case of British pilot treated at Tropical Diseases Hospital.

The number of cases confirmed to be positive again included patient number 207, patient number 51 (two Brazilian nationals, discharged on April 18) and patient 224 (discharged on April 20).



The British pilot repeatedly tested negative but later became positive again



Particularly for the case of a 43-year-old male pilot from the UK who shows no worse disease condition, the PCR test sample has become negative (the patient has repeatedly tested negative and then re-positive. calculated with SARS-CoV-2). By the morning of April 29, the patient had no fever, pulse and stable blood pressure, had ECMO, mechanical ventilation and dialysis, the prognosis was still severe.

There are 57 cases in the whole city, being isolated in the isolated areas of the city; 8 cases of isolation in isolated areas of districts; 94 cases of monitoring and isolation at home.

The Center for Disease Control said that to ensure safety and avoid the risk of disease entering the city, the testing sample continues to be maintained for passengers entering through international airports. . In addition, the entire flight crew of international flights staying in the city is closely monitored. Currently, the city has tested samples of 102 people, 92 with negative results, 10 people are waiting for results.

It is expected that in the near future, the city will welcome about 10,000 people to enter the country through Tan Son Nhat International Airport, the coordination to prepare the concentrated isolation areas is being promoted to best meet the basic conditions. for isolated people as well as health care services.
