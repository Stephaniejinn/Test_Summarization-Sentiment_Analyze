Title : Warren County sees slight increase in new COVID-19 cases
Ariticle : 
Warren County is now reporting 78 confirmed COVID-19 cases following a report Saturday afternoon by the Mississippi State Department of Health. This is an increase of four cases since Friday.
Overall, the state reported an additional 229 cases, far lower than the record-high one-day total it reported Friday.
The number of confirmed cases in Claiborne County is now 27.
The number of virus-related deaths in the state grew to 291, an increase of 10.
It is reporting the state is continuing to show that 3,413 of the total statewide cases are now "presumed recovered."
