Title : The last Covid – 19 patient was discharged from Ninh Binh General Hospital
Ariticle : 

In the morning of 4/5, the leader of Ninh Binh General Hospital said that after more than a month of treatment here, the situation of VTL patient (25 years old, Luc Nam district, Bac Giang province) infected with Covid-19 th 166 has stabilized.

The patient has a normal state, no cough, no fever, no difficulty breathing. After 4 recent tests (on 21, 23, 25 April and 2/5) all tested negative for SARS-CoV-2, the patient was electrically discharged.



The doctors from Ninh Binh General Hospital congratulate the last case of Covid-19 treated in the hospital.



Previous VTL patients, working in Thailand, after entry, were isolated in a military unit in Ninh Binh province, then tested positive for SARS-CoV-2.

On March 26, this patient was transferred to be treated at the Infectious Department, Ninh Binh General Hospital. Here, patients are treated with antibiotics, hypothermia, electrolyte rehydration, enhance immunity, improve fitness.

Thus, up to now, Ninh Binh General Hospital has cured 10 out of 10 COVID-19 patients.
