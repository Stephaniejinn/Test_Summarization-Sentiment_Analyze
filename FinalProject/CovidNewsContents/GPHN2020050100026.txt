Title : Algeria receives medical equipment donated by Russia
Ariticle : 
ALGIERS- Medical equipment offered by Russia, as a contribution to Algeria's efforts to stem the spread of the coronavirus (Covid-19), was received Wednesday at the air base of Boufarik, Blida (50-km south of Algiers), the ministry of National Defence said in a statement.
