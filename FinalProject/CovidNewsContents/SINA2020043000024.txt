Title : The number of deaths in the United States has reached the number Trump said
Ariticle : 
Original title: The number of deaths in the United States, to the number Trump said
A total of 1036652 cases of new coronary pneumonia were diagnosed in the United States, and the death toll exceeded 60,000.
According to the Global New Coronary Pneumonia Data Real-Time Statistics System released by Johns Hopkins University in the United States, as of 6 p.m. EST on April 29, the United States reported a total of 1036652 cases of new coronary pneumonia, 60475 deaths, and 6026107 cases.
In the past 24 hours, there were 25,935 new diagnoses and 2110 deaths in the United States.
According to U.S. media reports, some local governments are actively preparing for lifting the ban, allowing stores to resume business and schools to resume classes. The US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention and the US Environmental Protection Agency jointly announced on the 29th guidelines for cleaning and disinfection of reopening shops, offices, public places, and schools.
