Title : A 68-year-old man died from coronavirus in Maldonado
Ariticle : 
A 68-year-old man in Maldonado died of coronavirus on Wednesday night. This is the sixteenth fatal victim of this disease in our country, whose first records date back to March 13. According to the latest survey of the National Emergency System, there are 630 confirmed positive cases of Covid-19 coronavirus throughout the national territory (5 more than the number reported on Tuesday). Of that total, 412 have already recovered. The government yesterday presented the new data for Coronavirus Covid-19 in Uruguay. At a press conference by Luis Lacalle Pou, the president said that on Wednesday 876 analysis tests were made and 18 tested positive.
