Title : Myanamr's Kachin State among global IDPs vulnerable to COVID-19
Ariticle : 
There are millions of people around the world left homeless, living in camps who are getting little, if any support from governments in the face of the pandemic.
Governments around the world say they are doing what they can for their citizens affected by the coronavirus pandemic.
But there are millions of people left homeless, living in camps who are getting little, if any support.
Al Jazeera's Scott Heidler reports on one such community in northern Myanmar.
