Title : One new coronavirus case reported Saturday in Hawaii
Ariticle : 
One new case of COVID-19 was reported Saturday in Hawaii.
That brings the statewide number of reported cases to 620, according to the state Department of Health.
The new case was on Maui, which has had 117 cases.
The numbers for Hawaii County (73), Oahu (400) and Kauai County (21) remain unchanged.
There were no new deaths reported Saturay by the DOH. The statewide death toll remains 16.
So far, 72 cases statewide have required hospitalization, and 541 individuals have been released from isolation.
