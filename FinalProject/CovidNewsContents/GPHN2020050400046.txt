Title : Chinese mainland reports 2 new confirmed COVID-19 cases
Ariticle : 
BEIJING, May 3 (Xinhua) -- Chinese health authority said Sunday that it received reports of two new confirmed COVID-19 cases on the Chinese mainland Saturday, including an imported case in Shanghai and an indigenous case in Shanxi Province.
No deaths or suspected cases were reported Saturday on the mainland, the National Health Commission said in a daily report.
