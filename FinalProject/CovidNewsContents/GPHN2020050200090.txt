Title : No increase in COVID-19 cases in Huron Perth - My Stratford Now

No increase in COVID-19 cases in Huron Perth
SHARE ON:
Illustration of Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome coronavirus 2, the cause of COVID-19 (Alissa Eckert, MS, Dan Higgins, MAMS)
There has been no increase in the number of confirmed cases of COVID-19 in Huron Perth.
In the last 24 hours, Huron Perth Public Health says the number of confirmed cases has stayed flat at 47.
To date, 1,613 people have been tested for COVID-19 in the region with 1,231 testing negative and 335 still pending.
Five people have died from the virus in the region.
