Title : International Labor Day: portraits of workers in the fight against the COVID-19 epidemic
Ariticle : 
Peng Zhiqiang, head of the Chinese medical team in Serbia, helps a team member wear protective suit at a designated hospital for COVID-19 patients in Belgrade, Serbia, March 25, 2020. (Chinese medical expert team to Serbia/Handout via Xinhua)
Many people have been sticking to their posts as the world fights against the COVID-19 epidemic.
BEIJING, May 3 (Xinhua) -- The first of May is a national, public holiday in many countries across the world as International Labour Day. This year, the global fight against the COVID-19 epidemic endowed the day with a new meaning as ordinary people from all walks of life are taking part in the common fight with their own brave and selfless persistence.
