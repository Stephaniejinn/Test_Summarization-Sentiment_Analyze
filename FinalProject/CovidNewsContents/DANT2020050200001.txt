Title : 2 cases of Covid-19 related to Buddha bar were positive again
Ariticle : 

Ho Chi Minh City Department of Health said that two male patients with Covid-19 who had been to Buddha&#39;s shop had positive results for SARS-CoV-2 virus on the 15th day after discharge.

Thus, TP is currently treating for 7 cases, including 6 patients who re-tested positive.

2 new cases re-found positive were 124 patients and 235 patients treated at Cu Chi field hospital, Ho Chi Minh City. In particular, BN 124 is a male, 52 years old, a Brazilian national, residing in Ho Chi Minh City, working at Gia Dinh Shoe Co., Ltd., discharged from the hospital on April 14 after 3 negative results. And BN 235, male, 25 years old, British nationality. After the treatment process, the patient was tested and 3 times with negative results, and was discharged on April 15.

After being discharged from the hospital, these patients were subjected to an additional 14-day isolation period at home, undergone three physical examinations and tests on 5, 10, and 15 days after discharge. Thus, together with Patient 92, the two patients were re-positive for SARS-CoV-2 virus on the 15th day after discharge.

Because the patients were re-positive at different times after discharge, HCMC Department of Health decided to organize health monitoring for Covid-19 patients after discharge: testing for SARS-CoV-2 virus. every day for 30 days after discharge.

Currently, there are 33 cases with negative results after 14 days of discharge; 4 cases waited for samples to be taken 10 days after discharge and 4 cases waited for samples to take place 15 days after discharge. The total number of tests conducted by the city so far for all subjects is 55,994 people.
