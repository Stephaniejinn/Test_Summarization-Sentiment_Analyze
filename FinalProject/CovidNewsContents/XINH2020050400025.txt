Title : Chinese mainland reports no new domestically transmitted COVID-19 cases Sunday
Ariticle : 
People enjoy themselves in Xiangcheng District of Xiangyang City, central China's Hubei Province, April 29, 2020. (Xinhua/Xie Jianfei)
BEIJING, May 4 (Xinhua) -- Chinese health authority said Monday that no new domestically transmitted cases of the novel coronavirus disease (COVID-19) were reported on the Chinese mainland on Sunday.
The National Health Commission received reports of three new confirmed COVID-19 cases on the Chinese mainland on Sunday, all of which were imported.
One suspected case arriving from overseas was reported in Shanghai, the commission said in its daily report.
No deaths related to the disease were reported, according to the commission.
