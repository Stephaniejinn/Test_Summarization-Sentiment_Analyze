Title : Chinese mainland reports 4 new imported COVID-19 cases
Ariticle : 
Medical staff of Xiaotangshan Hospital disinfect a ward after the hospital cleared all COVID-19 cases in Beijing, capital of China, April 28, 2020. 
(Xinhua/Peng Ziyang)
BEIJING, April 30 (Xinhua) -- The Chinese mainland reported 4 new imported cases of the novel coronavirus disease (COVID-19) Wednesday, bringing the total number of imported cases to 1,664, the National Health Commission said Thursday.
Of the total imported cases, 1,139 had been discharged from hospitals after recovery, and 525 were being treated in hospitals with 13 in severe conditions, the commission said.
No deaths had been reported from the imported cases.
