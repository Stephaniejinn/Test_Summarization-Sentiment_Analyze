Title : 2 new imported COVID-19 cases reported in Shanghai
Ariticle : 
Two more imported case of novel coronavirus were reported in the city on Wednesday, while 13 imported cases — nine from Russia, three from the UK and one from Italy — were discharged from hospital upon recovery, said the Shanghai Health Commission on Thursday morning.
The new patients, one Chinese working in Singapore and one Chinese studying in the US, arrived at the Shanghai Pudong International Airport on April 27 and 28, respectively.
The newly diagnosed cases have been transferred to the designated hospital for treatment and 38 close contacts on the same flight have been tracked down and put under quarantine and observation.
So far, of all the 308 imported cases, 266 have been discharged upon recovery and 42 are still hospitalized, including two in critical condition.
Another six imported suspected cases are undergoing test.
No new locally transmitted cases or suspected cases were reported on Wednesday.
Of all the 339 local cases, 331 have been discharged upon recovery and one is hospitalized. There have been so far seven local fatalities.
No asymptomatic infection cases are undergoing medical observation right now, the health commission said.
