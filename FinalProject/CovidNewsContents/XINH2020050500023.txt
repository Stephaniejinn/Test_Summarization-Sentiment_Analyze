Title : Chinese mainland reports no new domestically transmitted COVID-19 cases
Ariticle : 
Tourists visit the Yellow Crane Tower, or Huanghelou, a landmark in Wuhan, central China's Hubei Province, April 29, 2020.
 (Xinhua/Xiao Yijiu)
BEIJING, May 5 (Xinhua) -- Chinese health authority said Tuesday that no new domestically transmitted cases of the novel coronavirus disease (COVID-19) were reported on the Chinese mainland for Monday.
One confirmed case arriving from overseas was reported in Shanghai Monday, the National Health Commission said in its daily report Tuesday.
No new suspected case or deaths related to the disease were reported, according to the commission.
