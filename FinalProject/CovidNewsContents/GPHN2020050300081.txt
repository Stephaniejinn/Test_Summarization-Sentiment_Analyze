Title : Pageland has 4 new cases of COVID-19
Ariticle : 
Four more COVID-19 cases reported in Pageland on Friday with the number of active cases in Chesterfield County at 27.
The S.C. Department of Health and Environmental Control reported seven new cases in the county. In addition to the Pageland cases there were new cases in Cheraw, Jefferson and McBee.
The active cases by ZIP code are seven in Cheraw, seven in Chesterfield, five in Pageland, three in Jefferson, two in Patrick, two in Ruby and one in McBee.
DHEC estimates there are 356 possible cases in the county for a total of 414.
One Chesterfield County resident, a 75-year-old man, has died from the virus.
Statewide, there were 160 new cases released Friday, with 12 deaths.
Since DHEC starting tracking the virus in March there have been 6,258 confirmed cases and 160 deaths.
South Carolina's recovery rate for confirmed cases is 78 percent, according to DHEC.
The percentage of people receiving a negative test for the virus is 78 percent, according to DHEC.
