Title : China's help "of essential importance" in Serbia's fight against pandemic: president
Ariticle : 


   China's help was "of essential importance "in Serbia's fight against the COVID-19 pandemic, said Serbian President Aleksandar Vucic on Friday.He made the remarks at a press conference after meeting Chinese Ambassador to Serbia Chen Bo, China's medical expert team, and the representatives of Chinese biotech company BGI at the Palace of Serbia.
