Title : Chinese military medics help Myanmar military build COVID-19 testing laboratory
Ariticle : 


A team of medical personnel from the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA) on Thursday assisted the Myanmar military in building a laboratory for testing the COVID-19.The military medical team from the PLA, along with emergency medical supplies, arrived in Yangon on April 24 to assist in Myanmar's COVID-19 fight.The laboratory will start operation next week.
