Title : Beijing's SARS treatment hospital clears all COVID-19 cases
Ariticle : 


Beijing's SARS treatment hospital cleared all COVID-19 cases as the last two patients with COVID-19 were discharged Tuesday morning.Xiaotangshan Hospital, which was previously used to quarantine SARS patients in Beijing, is scheduled to cease operation on Wednesday.Over the past 44 days, the hospital has screened a total of 2,175 people, and 54 confirmed cases of COVID-19 were reported.
