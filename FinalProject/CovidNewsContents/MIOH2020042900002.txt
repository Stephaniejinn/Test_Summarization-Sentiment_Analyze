Title : Health enables over 497 ICU beds in the fight against coronavirus
Ariticle : 
In all, 2,258 ICU beds have been activated for patients with COVID-19. Ministry of Health transfers for each bed twice the normal value, totaling R $ 327.1 million
The populations of the states of Alagoas, Pernambuco and Goiás won more reinforcement to combat coronaviruses. On Monday (27), the Ministry of Health has enabled over 497 ICU beds, with 5 of them pediatric ICU to meet exclusively serious or critical patients of the disease. The ordinances with the publications are now available in the Official Gazette (DOU). With these publications, the total enabled beds in Brazil, only in April, it reaches 2,258, with 26 of them pediatric ICU.

In all, 21 states have received reinforcement and money to provide better assistance to the population in combating the pandemic, totaling R $ 327.1 million in federal funds already sent to states and municipalities. Ensuring the transfer of resources is given through clearance granted by folder, exceptionally, for a period of 90 days and may be extended as long as public health emergency in the country due to COVID-19. For each bed will be paid R $ 1600 per night the ICU bed, double what is normally passed. This is another reinforcement of the Union to the states in dealing with the coronavirus pandemic.
The states covered so far are: Acre, Alagoas, Bahia, Ceará, Distrito Federal, Espírito Santo, Goiás, Maranhão, Minas Gerais, Mato Grosso do Sul, Pará, Pernambuco, Piauí, Paraná, Rio de Janeiro, Rio Grande do Norte , Rondônia, Rio Grande do Sul, Santa Catarina, Sergipe and Sao Paulo. Other qualifications for Brazil are already being analyzed by folder.
The authorization request for the costing is done by local managers to ensure the framework for operation of the beds and the Ministry of Health transfers the value that is intended to maintain the services.
Earlier this month the Ministry of Health published
a
Ordinance No. 568 of 26 March 2020 which doubled the value of the daily cost of ICU beds Adult and Pediatric R $ 800 to R $ 1600, exceptionally, exclusively for the care of patients with coronavirus. Thus, these beds enabled temporarily have begun to receive the differentiated value of the incentive.
