Title : Xinhua Photos of the Day (May 4)
Ariticle : 
Beijing, May 4 (Xinhua) -- The world in the past 24hrs. A selection of the best daily press photos from Xinhua.
A mountain re-measuring team depart from the base camp of Mount Qomolangma in southwest China's Tibet Autonomous Region in this aerial photo taken on May 2, 2020.
 Since the founding of the People's Republic of China in 1949, Chinese surveyors have conducted six rounds of scaled measurement and scientific research on Mount Qomolangma and released the height of the peak twice in 1975 and 2005, which was 8,848.13 meters and 8,844.43 meters, respectively. (Xinhua/Sun Fei)
 Aerial photo taken on April 30, 2020 shows the central Rongbuk Glacier at the foot of Mount Qomolangma, southwest China's Tibet Autonomous Region.
 With an altitude of about 5,400 meters, the central Rongbuk Glacier is scattered with seracs, ice bridges and ice needles, forming a spectacular view with its diverse bounties. (Xinhua/Dainzin Nyima Choktrul)
A woman wearing protective gear is seen on street in Zhengzhou, central China's Henan Province, May 3, 2020. 
Zhengzhou meteorological authorities on Sunday issued a red alert for high temperature. (Xinhua/Zhu Xiang)
Isabel Diaz Ayuso (C), the President of the Madrid Region, and civil care workers observe a moment of silence for COVID-19 victims at the Puerta del Sol square in Madrid, Spain, May 2, 2020. (Madrid Region Government/Handout via Xinhua)
Photo taken on May 3, 2020 shows statues wearing masks at the Trocadero esplanade in Paris, France, during the lockdown in France to stop the spread of COVID-19.
 The French government has decided to extend the state of health emergency until July 24 to fight against the coronavirus pandemic, Minister of Health Olivier Vrran announced on Saturday. (Photo by Aurelien Morissard/Xinhua)
Medical workers are busy in an intensive care unit for patients with coronavirus infection in the hospital of the National Medical Research Center for Cardiovascular Surgery of the Russian Ministry of Health in Moscow, Russia, May 2, 2020. (Sputnik via Xinhua)
Customers with face masks shop at a stationery store in Johannesburg, South Africa, May 2, 2020. 
(Xinhua/Chen Cheng)
Indian policemen on motorcycles pay tribute to doctors, nurses and police personnel who battle against COVID-19 pandemic in New Delhi, India, May 3, 2020. (Photo by Javed Dar/Xinhua)
People view cherry blossoms in Rongcheng, east China's Shandong Province, May 3, 2020. (Photo by Li Xinjun/Xinhua)
People dance in a park in Hefei, east China's Anhui Province, May 3, 2020. (Xinhua/Zhang Duan)
Combo photo taken on May 3, 2020 shows scaffolder Liu Yuankui installing scaffolds (left) and posing for a photo at the construction site of the Renhuai-Zunyi expressway in Zunyi, southwest China's Guizhou Province. Liu, 23, from Weining County of Guizhou, has been working as a scaffolder for more than 2 years.
 (Xinhua/Liu Xu)
 A staff member of Luoyang Institute of Science and Technology dries students' bedding as a preparation to welcome them back to school in Luoyang, central China's Henan Province, May 3, 2020. (Photo by Huang Zhengwei/Xinhua)
People enjoy leisure time in Beirut, Lebanon, May 3, 2020. (Photo by Bilal Jawich/Xinhua)
