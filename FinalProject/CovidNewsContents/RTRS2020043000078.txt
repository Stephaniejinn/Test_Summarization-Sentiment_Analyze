Title : England hospital death toll rises 391 to 20,137
Ariticle : 
LONDON (Reuters) - A further 391 people who tested positive for the coronavirus in English hospitals have died, taking the total there to 20,137, health officials said.  
Within the 391, 15 of the patients aged between 49 and 97 had no known underlying health condition.
