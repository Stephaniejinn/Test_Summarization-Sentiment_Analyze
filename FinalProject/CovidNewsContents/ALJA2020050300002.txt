Title : India Muslims targeted in attacks over coronavirus
Ariticle : 
Muslims in India have been attacked after a religious gathering in the capital allegedly led to a rise in coronavirus cases, with the minority being blamed for the spread of the disease.
India has rejected a report by the US Commission on International Religious Freedom, which named it "a country of particular concern".
