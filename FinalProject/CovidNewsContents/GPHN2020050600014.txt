Title : Lebanon extends public mobilization against coronavirus
Ariticle : 
BEIRUT, May 5 (KUNA) -- The Lebanese cabinet decided to extend until May 24 the state of public mobilization against the novel coronavirus (Covid-19) pandemic. The government will pursue its efforts to contain the spread of the pandemic, the cabinet said in a press release read by Minister of Information Manal Abdul-Samad on Tuesday. Earlier today, the Lebanese Ministry of Health said one person contracted coronavirus, bringing the overall infections to 741, with the death toll remaining unchanged at 25.
