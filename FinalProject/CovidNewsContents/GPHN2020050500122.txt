Title : China reports zero deaths, 1 'imported' coronavirus case
Ariticle : 
KUWAIT, May 5 (KUNA) -- Chinese health authorities on Tuesday reported only one imported infection with the novel coronavirus (COVID-19), with zero fatalities.
Mainland China's total number of infections now stands at 82,881, with the death toll still standing at 4,633, the National Health Commission said in a statement, carried by Xinhua news agency. (end) mab.hb
