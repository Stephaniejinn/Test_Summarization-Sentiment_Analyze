Title : Gov't building in Indian capital sealed after official tests positive for COVID-19

NEW DELHI, April 29 (Xinhua) -- The building of the Indian government's top policy think tank, the National Institution for Transforming India (NITI) Aayog (commission), was sealed Tuesday in Delhi after an official tested positive for the COVID-19, officials said.
"An employee working at NITI Bhavan has been detected positive with the COVID-19. It was informed to the authorities at 9:00 (local time) this morning. NITI Aayog is following all the due protocols necessary as per the ministry of health guidelines. The building has been sealed," read a statement issued by NITI Aayog.
According to officials, the sanitization of the building was underway.
"Disinfection and sanitisation of the building are underway. Contacts of the single COVID-positive person have been asked to go on self-quarantine," the Aayog said.
Officials said the building would be closed for two days.
On Tuesday morning, the number of COVID-19 cases in the country reached 29,435 including 934 deaths.
