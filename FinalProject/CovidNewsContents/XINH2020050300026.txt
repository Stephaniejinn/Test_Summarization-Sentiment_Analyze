Title : Asia Album: Sri Lankans stranded in capital district gather for special public buses to go back home for holiday
Ariticle : 
Medical officers check the temperature of passengers before they board special public buses to go back home for holiday, at the Peliyagoda grounds in Colombo district, Sri Lanka, May 2, 2020. (Xinhua/Ajith Perera)
COLOMBO, May 3 (Xinhua) -- Over 50,000 Sri Lankan people gathered Saturday for special public buses at the Peliyagoda grounds in the Colombo district to go back home ahead of the Vesak religious festival which falls on May 7. Thousands of people have been stranded in the Colombo district due to a district to district travel ban imposed since March to prevent the spread of COVID-19 pandemic.
