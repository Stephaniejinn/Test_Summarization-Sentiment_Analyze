Title : Indonesia reports 349 new coronavirus cases, 14 new deaths
Ariticle : 
JAKARTA (Reuters) - Indonesia reported 349 new coronavirus cases, taking the total number of infections in the Southeast Asian country to 11,192, health ministry official Achmad Yurianto said on Sunday. 
Yurianto said 14 new coronavirus-related deaths were confirmed, bringing the total to 845.  
More than 83,000 people have been tested for the virus, he added.
