Title : Germany's confirmed coronavirus cases rise by 1,639; deaths up 193: RKI
Ariticle : 
BERLIN (Reuters) - The number of confirmed coronavirus cases in Germany increased by 1,639 to 160,758, data from the Robert Koch Institute (RKI) for infectious diseases showed on Friday. 
The death toll rose by 193 to 6,481.
