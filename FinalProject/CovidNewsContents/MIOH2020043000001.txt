Title : Brazil recorded 78,162 cases of coronavirus and 5,466 deaths from the disease
Ariticle : 
The information was updated by State Health Departments to 14h on Wednesday (29). Of the 78,162 cases that are confirmed, 34,132 are recovered (44%) and 38 564 were in attendance
The Ministry of Health reported 78,162 cases of coronavirus and 5,466 deaths from the disease in Brazil until 14h on Wednesday (29), according to information forwarded by the State Health Departments across the country. In the last 24 hours, there were 6,276 new cases and 449 new deaths. Of the 78,162 cases that are confirmed, 34,132 are recovered (44%) and 38,564 were in attendance. There are still 1,452 deaths that are under investigation. The data were presented by the Minister of Health, Nelson Teich, during a hearing by videoconference of the Senate on the afternoon of Wednesday (29).
Currently, all states recorded cases and deaths from coronavirus. São Paulo concentrates most of the reports, with 26,158 cases and 2,247 deaths. Rio de Janeiro is in second place, with 8,869 cases and 794 deaths. The state registers fewer notifications is Tocantins, with 116 records and three deaths.
