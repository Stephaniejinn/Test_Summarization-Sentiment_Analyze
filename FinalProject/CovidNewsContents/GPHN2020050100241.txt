Another three cases of COVID-19 have been confirmed by Wellington Dufferin Guelph Public Health in the past 24 hours.
There are now 274 cases in the region. One of the new cases is in Wellington County, where there are now a total of 45 confirmed cases.
However, 19 of those have been resolved. Two people have died in the county.
