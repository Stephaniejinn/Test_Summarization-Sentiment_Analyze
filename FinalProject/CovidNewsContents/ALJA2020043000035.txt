Title : Afghanistan forced to release prisoners as healthcare collapses
Ariticle : 
Coronavirus adds more strain to healthcare system already battered by decades of conflict and political instability.
Afghanistan's healthcare system has been battered by decades of conflict and political instability.
The new 
coronavirus pandemic
 is putting more strain on its medical workers.
Now, the government is releasing prisoners to stop the virus from spreading in its cramped prisons and to prevent hospitals from becoming even more overwhelmed.
Al Jazeera's Priyanka Gupta reports.
