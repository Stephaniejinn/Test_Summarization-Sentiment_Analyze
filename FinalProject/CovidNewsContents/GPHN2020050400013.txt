Title : Latest coronavirus statistics for borough
Ariticle : 
THE latest number of confirmed coronavirus cases in the borough is 694, say government figures.
The latest statistics, released on Saturday, May 2 recorded 694 confirmed cases across the borough.
There have been 166 people confirmed to have died after they tested positive for COVID-19 at Wrightington, Wigan and Leigh NHS Trust.
Nationally, as of Saturday's figures there have been 182,260 confirmed cases, with 28,131 people having died after testing positive.
