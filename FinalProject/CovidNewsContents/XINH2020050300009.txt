Title : Just In: Italy registers 209,328 coronavirus cases, death toll at 28,710
Ariticle : 
ROME, May 2 (Xinhua) -- The coronavirus pandemic has claimed 28,710 lives in locked-down Italy, bringing the total number of infections, fatalities, and recoveries so far to 209,328, according to the latest data released by the country's Civil Protection Department on Saturday.
