Title : Over 15,000 Persons Screened by Bani Malik Health Sector
Ariticle : 






30 April 2020



Page Content
​The Healthcare Centers Affairs Department of Bani Malik Health Sector continues the implementation of various precautionary measures to stop the outbreak of Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19). Tow rapid intervention teams have been formed by the Sector, trained and enhanced with necessary equipment to assume their tasks in the Active Surveillance Program for workers’ houses.
It is noteworthy that, the Visual Triage Stations have screened 15,328 persons since the start of these measures. Also, the medical teams of Bani Malik Health Sector have conducted a number of visits according to the Program, covering 22 houses with 568 workers screened. In collaboration with the competent authorities in the governorate, one house (encompassing 21 workers) has been evacuated for non-compliance with health regulations, and the workers have been transferred to another house compatible with such health regulations.
