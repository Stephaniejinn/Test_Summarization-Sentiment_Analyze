TTO - COVID-19 test results of 87 apartment residents where patient 92 has all tested negative for the new strain of corona virus. 5 nearby convenience store staff also had negative results.

The Ho Chi Minh City Center for Disease Control on May 1 said the results showed that 92 patients did not spread the disease to those around them. With
As a result, people in the apartment can live normally but still have to monitor their health, wear masks, wash their hands frequently, and limit close contact with others.
Particularly for households on the upper floor, there are apartments of resident patients, the health sector recommends that people continue to isolate at home, monitor health, closely monitor in the coming days.
According to the reporter, 1-5 noon, police force, urban order, civil defense ... Da Kao ward, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City are still on duty, blockade apartment B1-A1 Nguyen Dinh Chieu.
The way in and out of block B1 is blockaded by iron barrier with posted sign of isolation area.
Block B1 has 5 floors with 36 households. After the patient tested positive for COVID-19 again, all samples were tested and isolated.
Earlier ivory
On April 29, the Centers for Disease Control in Ho Chi Minh City discovered the 15th day sample of 92 patients who tested positive for corona virus. Previously, this patient had been sampled two times on the 5th and 10th days, all were negative.
This patient rents an apartment at 1A-1B apartment on Nguyen Dinh Chieu street, Da Kao ward, district 1 and strictly abides the isolation, not contacting people in the apartment.
After 2 negative tests on the 5th and 10th days after discharge, he went to the convenience store to buy some personal items in a very short time, when he wore a mask.
As soon as he got a positive result, the Centers for Disease Control of Ho Chi Minh City and District 1 Health Center transferred him back to Cu Chi Field War Hospital to isolate treatment and at the same time investigate the epidemic. from the contact cases, taking samples for emergency contact cases, all the people of the apartments in the apartment building and sanitizing the whole area.
