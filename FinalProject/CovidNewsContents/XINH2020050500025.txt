Title : All junior and senior high schools reopen in Guiyang, SW China

GUIYANG, May 5 (Xinhua) -- Junior and senior high schools reopen in Guiyang, southwest China's Guizhou Province.
After students in their last year at junior and senior high schools returned to schools on March 16, all the other high school students in Guiyang resumed classes on May 4 with strict prevention measures against COVID-19.
