Title : Kenya: Amason Kingi Declares Kilifi COVID-19 Free
Ariticle : 
Kilifi Governor Amason Kingi has declared the county coronavirus-free for now after last six patients who had tested positive were cured and discharged.
They were admitted to Jibana Health Centre.
