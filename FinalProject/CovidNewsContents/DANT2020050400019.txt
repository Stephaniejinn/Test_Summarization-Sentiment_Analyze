Title : Afternoon 4/5: No cases of Covid-19 in the community
Ariticle : 

From 6am to 6pm on 4/5, Vietnam recorded 0 new cases. Thus, there have been no new cases detected in the country since 18 days.

Total close contacts and entry from epidemic areas are under health monitoring (isolation): 27.409, of which:

- Isolator concentration in hospital: 238

- Isolation concentrated in other establishments: 5,871

- Isolation at home and place of residence: 21,300


Regarding the treatment situation, according to the report of the Treatment Sub-Committee of the National Steering Committee for Disease Prevention and Control Covid-19:

- 2 patients were declared cured:

+ Patient 170 at Kim Son District General Hospital, Ninh Binh Province.

+ Patient 166 at Ninh Binh General Hospital.

- Number of negative cases for the first time with SARS-CoV-2: 10 cases.

- Number of negative cases 2 or more with SARS-CoV-2: 11 cases.

According to information from the treatment subcommittee, the National Steering Committee for prevention and control of Covid-19 epidemic, on 4/5, 1 patient with Covid-19 at Ninh Binh General Hospital was announced to be cured. This is the last case of Covid-19 among 10 patients treated in Ninh Binh General Hospital so far.


So far, Vietnam has recorded 271 cases of Covid-19, of which 219 patients were cured and discharged.

Vietnam has succeeded in controlling the disease but its risk remains high. According to experts in the Covid-19 epidemic there are many asymptomatic cases, cases of mild fever, cough just like the flu and it can exist in the community and spread. Because of this nature, the epidemic has a prolonged, unresolved spread.

In particular, in our country there is still a risk of epidemic penetration and probable cases in the community.
