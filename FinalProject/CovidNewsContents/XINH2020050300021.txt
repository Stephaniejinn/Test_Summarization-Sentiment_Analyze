Title : Chinese mainland reports 1 new imported COVID-19 case
Ariticle : 
Staff members of the customs check the health declaration information of newly arrived inbound passengers in Chongqing, southwest China, March 29, 2020. 
 (Xinhua/Huang Wei)
BEIJING, May 3 (Xinhua) -- The Chinese mainland reported one new imported case of the novel coronavirus disease (COVID-19) Saturday, bringing the total number of imported cases to 1,672, the National Health Commission said Sunday.
Of the total imported cases, 1,221 had been discharged from hospitals after recovery, and 451 were being treated in hospitals with 6 in severe conditions, the commission said.
No deaths had been reported from the imported cases.
