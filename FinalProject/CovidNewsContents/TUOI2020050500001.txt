Title : The patient in Henan died of end-stage cirrhosis, not because of COVID-19
Ariticle : 

TTO - On the evening of 4-5, the National Steering Committee for COVID-19 disease prevention and control said that 251 patients who were negative for COVID-19, were transferred to the local hospital for monitoring, just died on 2-5 due to end-stage cirrhosis.


Specifically, patient No. 251 is NVD. (64 years old, in Ha Nam) treated at Ha Nam General Hospital from March 20, to 7-4 patients who tested positive. The patient is a high-risk patient due to advanced age, many underlying diseases (cirrhosis, gout, alcoholism) and could not find the source of infection for the patient.
After a period of treatment at Ha Nam General Hospital, the patient was transferred to the National Hospital of Tropical Diseases, the treatment here was stable, has been confirmed negative 4 times with SARS-CoV-2, was transferred to Ha Nam to watch on April 24.
However, the patient died from early morning 2-5 due to end-stage cirrhosis.
According to the National Steering Committee for COVID-19 disease prevention, patients did not die from COVID-19 but due to underlying disease and exhaustion.
However, this is the first patient ever associated with COVID-19 to die.
To 4-5 days, Vietnam recorded 271 patients COVID-19, not recorded deaths due to COVID-19.
