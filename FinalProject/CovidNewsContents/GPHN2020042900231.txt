Title : Beijing city lowers COVID-19 emergency response level
Ariticle : 
Special: Battle Against Novel Coronavirus
The Chinese capital will lower its emergency response to the novel coronavirus epidemic from the top level to the second one starting April 30, a local official announced Wednesday.
