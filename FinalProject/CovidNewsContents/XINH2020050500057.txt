Title : Israeli PM announces ease of COVID-19 rules
Ariticle : 


Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu announced on Monday a relaxation of additional COVID-19 restrictions in Israel.He hailed the anti-virus progress showed by a declining number of new infections and an increasing number of recoveries.The new rules cancels the 100-0meter-from-home restriction and allow visits by first-degree relatives and gatherings of up to 20 people.
