Title : World has right to know when virus started in U.S.: China's FM spokesperson
Ariticle : 


"Why not ask US experts to locate when the virus first started in the US? American people need answers. The world also has a right to know," Chinese Foreign Ministry spokeswoman Hua Chunying tweeted in response to U.S. Secretary of State Mike Pompeo's smear on China over the COVID-19 pandemic's source and handling.
