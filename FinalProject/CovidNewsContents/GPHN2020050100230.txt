Title : County's coronavirus case count grows by 16
Ariticle : 
As of Thursday, there were 366 confirmed coronavirus cases reported in Larimer County, according to data provided by the county health department. The number of cases was up 16 from the total cases reported Wednesday. According to statewide data, 19 people in the county have died due to COVID-19.
Neighboring Weld County has reported 1,776 cases and 95 deaths.
Latest Colorado cases, deaths
In Colorado, cases grew by 526 to 15,284 with 777 deaths and 2,697 total hospitalizations recorded, according to the state health department's Thursday afternoon update.
There have been 159 outbreaks at residential and nonhospital health care facilities in Colorado.
Hospital outlook
As of Thursday, 88% of facilities had reported data to the state. At those facilities, 756 people are currently hospitalized with confirmed COVID-19. In the past 24 hours, 58 patients had been discharged or transferred.
Four facilities are anticipating personal protective equipment shortages in the next week. No hospitals are anticipating ICU bed shortages in the next seven days. Of Colorado's 1,086 adult ventilators, 429 were in use Thursday.
Protect yourself
Health officials are advising everyone, especially those at high risk, to:
Stay home as much as possible.
Use common-sense precautions of covering coughs and sneezes, frequent hand-washing and avoiding touching your face.
Wear a cloth mask or face covering when leaving home.
