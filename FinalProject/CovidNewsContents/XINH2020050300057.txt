Title : Tunisia's Sidi Bou Said sit empty amid pandemic
Ariticle : 


Sidi Bou Said, a famous attraction in the capital Tunis noted for its white walls, blue doors and windows, has not received foreign tourists for many weeks. Tunisia's tourism industry has been severely affected by COVID-19 after all transportation suspended in early March.
