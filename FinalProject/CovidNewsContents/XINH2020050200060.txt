Title : U.S. draws criticism for defunding WHO amid world health crisis
Ariticle : 


As COVID-19 continues to take its toll globally, the U.S. government decided to halt funding to the WHO.The U.S. government claimed that "WHO failed to adequately share information in a timely and transparent fashion." In fact, the WHO has been closely following the situation, publishing information timely, and keeping countries worldwide on high alert against the virus.
