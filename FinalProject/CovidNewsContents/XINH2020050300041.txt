Title : Xinhua Photos of the Day (May 3)
Ariticle : 
Beijing, May 3 (Xinhua) -- The world in the past 24hrs. A selection of the best daily press photos from Xinhua.
Blue sheep are seen in Mount Qomolangma National Nature Reserve in southwest China's Tibet Autonomous Region, April 29, 2020. (Xinhua/Purbu Zhaxi)
People dance by Tangqung Co Lake in Nagqu, southwest China's Tibet Autonomous Region, April 30, 2020. (Xinhua/Hou Jie)
People take part in a marathon in an effort to raise funds for PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) for medical workers in local hospitals and nursing homes, in Prestatyn, north Wales, Britain, May 1, 2020. (Photo by Jon Super/Xinhua)
People applaud for medical workers on a residential building in Saint-Mande, near Paris, France, May 2, 2020. (Photo by Aurelien Morissard/Xinhua)
A formation of the Thunderbirds and Blue Angels fly over the National Mall in Washington D.C., the United States, May 2, 2020. The U.S. Navy's Blue Angels and U.S. Air Force's Thunderbirds honored frontline COVID-19 responders and essential workers with formation flights over here on Saturday. (Photo by Ting Shen/Xinhua)
People view blooming azalea at Longquan Mountain scenic spot in Danzhai County, southwest China's Guizhou Province, May 2, 2020. (Xinhua/Yang Ying)
Photo taken on May 2, 2020 shows blossoming jacaranda trees along a street in Kunming City, southwest China's Yunnan Province. (Xinhua/Lin Yiguang)
Photo taken on early May 2, 2020 shows a fire in Goseong, Gangwon Province, South Korea. A fire broke out late Friday in Goseong, Gangwon Province, forcing hundreds of people to evacuate. The fire has been mostly extinguished by Saturday noon, with no reports of casualties. (Photo by Lee Sang-ho/Xinhua)
Police officers wearing face masks block a road in Istanbul, Turkey, May 1, 2020. Turkish police on Friday detained at least 38 people who intended to stage several unauthorized marches to central Istanbul to mark the International Workers' Day, local media reported. (Photo by Mustafa Kaya/Xinhua)
Two women maintain social distance while a runner passes by in Barcelona, Spain, May 2, 2020. The Spanish government set time slots for outdoor activities, in line with social-distancing regulations, to prevent further contagion from COVID-19. (Photo by Joan Gosa/Xinhua)
 Liverpool Football Club's Anfield Stadium is locked and empty as sport events continue to be suspended during the COVID-19 pandemic in Liverpool, Britain, May 2, 2020. (Photo by Jon Super/Xinhua)
A family photo is seen in an abandoned house during clashes between fighters of the UN-backed Government of National Accord (GNA) and Libyan National Army (LNA) troops at Salah Al-Din frontline in Tripoli, Libya, May 2, 2020. (Photo by Amru Salahuddien/Xinhua)
Portuguese fado singer Cuca Roseta performs at a live concert on top of a vehicle running through streets in Lisbon, Portugal, May 1, 2020, amid the COVID-19 pandemic. (Photo by Pedro Fiuza/Xinhua)
People drive kartings in Anshan, northeast China's Liaoning Province during the May Day Holiday, May 2, 2020. (Xinhua/Yao Jianfeng)
Aerial photo taken on May 2, 2020 shows terraced fields in Rongjiang County, southwest China's Guizhou Province. (Photo by Wang Bingzhen/Xinhua)
Aerial photo taken on May 2, 2020 shows rice fields in Wenyu Village of Xiuying District in Haikou, south China's Hainan Province. (Xinhua/Pu Xiaoxu)
 Aerial photo taken on May 2, 2020 shows the night scene of Hinggan League Gymnasium in Ulanhot City, north China's Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region. (Xinhua/Xu Qin)
