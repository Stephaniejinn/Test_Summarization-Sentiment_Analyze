Title : Russian Prime Minister Mishustin&#39;s new crown virus nucleic acid test was positive
Ariticle : 
Original title: Russian Prime Minister Mishustin&#39;s new crown virus nucleic acid test was positive
On April 30 local time, Russian Prime Minister Mishustin said in a video connection with President Putin that he had received a nucleic acid test for the new coronavirus and the result was positive.
Mishustin proposed that the first deputy prime minister, Belousov, perform the duties of prime minister during his house separation.
Earlier, according to the information released by the official website of the Russian new crown virus epidemic prevention agency, as of 10:35 on April 30 local time, in the past 24 hours, there were 7099 new cases of new coronary pneumonia diagnosed in Russia, with a total of more than 100,000 cases, reaching 106498 cases. There were 1333 newly discharged cases cured, 11619 cases were cured, and 1073 cases died. In the capital city of Moscow, 3093 new cases were diagnosed, with a total of 53739 cases, making it the most severely affected area in the country. (Reporter Wang Bin)
