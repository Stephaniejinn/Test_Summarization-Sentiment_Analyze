Title : New virus cases nationwide fall to 28.(Thailand News Today--Monday, April)
Ariticle : 
Health officials confirmed 28 new cases of the Covid-19 coronavirus in the country today as the trend continues down.
Today's total brings the reported country total to 2,579 since the outbreak began in January.
There were also 2 additional deaths announced today, bringing the national tally to 40. Today's new infections are fewer than yesterday's 33, marking it the fifth consecutive day of declines since a spike of 111 cases on April 8.
Of today's cases, 12 are in Bangkok, 6 in Phuket, 2 each in Chon Buri, Satun and Yala provinces, and Nonthaburi, Nakhon Phanom and Chumphon each had 1 case.
