Title : China's Heilongjiang reports 5 new confirmed COVID-19 cases
Ariticle : 
A woman carries out volunteer work at a COVID-19 checkpoint at one of the entrances to the city of Suifenhe, northeast China's Heilongjiang Province, April 28, 2020. 
(Photo by Zhang Tao/Xinhua)
HARBIN, May 1 (Xinhua) -- Northeast China's Heilongjiang Province reported five new confirmed COVID-19 cases Thursday, all of whom were previously imported asymptomatic cases, the provincial health commission said Friday.
The province saw no new imported confirmed COVID-19 cases on Thursday.
By Thursday, the province had reported a total of 558 locally-transmitted confirmed COVID-19 cases and 386 imported cases.
The province had a total of nine imported asymptomatic cases by Thursday.
