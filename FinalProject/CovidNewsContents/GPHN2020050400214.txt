Title : 74 new cases of cororavirus in borough confirmed in latest figures
Ariticle : 
ANOTHER 74 cases of coronavirus have been confirmed in the borough in the latest statistics.
Public Health England figures show that 768 people had been confirmed as testing positive for COVID-19 by 9am Sunday morning (May 3).
Saturday's figure was 694.
The number was 670 at the same time on Friday, meaning the weekend figures saw a rise of 98.
A week before, there were 565 cases.
It means cases have increased by 15 per cent over the weekend, higher than the rate of increase across the UK of five per cent.
They were among the 20,125 cases recorded across the North West, a figure which rose by 1,182 over the period.
Cumulative case counts include patients who are currently unwell, have recovered and those that have died.
Across the UK, 186,599 people had tested positive for coronavirus as of 9am on Sunday, up from 177,454 at the same point on Friday, Department of Health and Social Care figures show. In total, 882,343 people had been tested, and, as of 5pm on Saturday, 28,446 had died.
