Title : Robot chefs cook food in China's restaurant
Ariticle : 


Try the delicious stir-fry cooked by robot chefs! A "smart restaurant" in east China's Jinan employs three robot chefs to help make safe and good food.The coronavirus outbreak is reshaping China's catering industry. More and more restaurant owners are fighting their way through the difficult time by using robots, offering takeouts and online promotion.
