Title : Xinhua Photos of the Day (April 30)
Ariticle : 
Beijing, April 30 (Xinhua) -- The world in the past 24hrs. A selection of the best daily press photos from Xinhua.
Tourists visit the Yellow Crane Tower, or Huanghelou, a landmark in Wuhan, central China's Hubei Province, April 29, 2020.
 As the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic wanes, Wuhan's landmark Yellow Crane Tower was partly reopened to public on Wednesday. For the time being, there is still a visitor number limit (300 tourists are allowed into the main building every half hour) and online booking is needed. (Xinhua/Xiao Yijiu)
Photo taken on April 15, 2020 shows a golden seal excavated from the Jiangkou stretch of the Minjiang River in Meishan City, southwest China's Sichuan Province. More than 10,000 relics, mainly gold and silver wares, have been excavated from the Jiangkou stretch of the Minjiang River, local authorities said on Wednesday. The site which dates back to the end of the Ming Dynasty (1368-1644) was listed as one of "China's top 10 archaeological discoveries in 2017". (Xinhua/Liu Kun)
People wait to receive bread provided by a charity bakery in Sanaa, Yemen, April 27, 2020. (Photo by Mohammed Mohammed/Xinhua)
Photo taken on April 28, 2020 shows Lake Burley Griffin in thick fog in Canberra, Australia. (Photo by Liu Changchang/Xinhua)
An employee of CRRC Changchun Railway Vehicles Co., Ltd. works on a new-generation middle-to-low-speed maglev train in Changchun, northeast China's Jilin Province, April 29, 2020. The new middle-to-low-speed maglev train can run at a speed of up to 120 kilometers per hour, and will be in operation in a maglev line in the city of Qingyuan in south China's Guangdong Province, according to the CRRC Changchun Railway Vehicles. (Xinhua/Xu Chang)
A girl walks a dog in Benxi, northeast China's Liaoning Province, April 29, 2020. (Xinhua/Yao Jianfeng)
Aerial photo taken on April 29, 2020 shows the Feilonghu Wujiang River Bridge under construction in southwest China's Guizhou Province. The two parts of the steel-truss cable suspension bridge joined together on Wednesday. The bridge, with a main span of 680 meters, is a key project of the Zunyi-Yuqing expressway. (Xinhua/Liu Xu)
A farmer burns flowers due to lack of market demand during a lockdown imposed by government at a flower farm in Kathmandu, capital of Nepal, April 28, 2020. The Nepali government on April 26 decided to extend the nationwide lockdown until May 7 to contain the spread of COVID-19 in the country. (Photo by Sunil Sharma/Xinhua)
A farmer separates the wheat from the chaff during COVID-19 lockdown in Jammu, winter capital of Indian-controlled Kashmir, April 29, 2020. (Str/Xinhua)
Violoncellist Li Yifan rehearses for an upcoming online orchestral concert at the Stele Forest, a museum for steles and stone sculptures, in Xi'an, northwest China's Shaanxi Province, April 28, 2020.
 The concert, to be livestreamed on May 2, will present the audience the charms of both classical music and Chinese cultural relics. (Xinhua/Li Yibo)
Photo taken on April 28, 2020 shows the Longmen Grottoes scenic area illuminated in Luoyang, central China's Henan Province.
 The Longmen Grottoes scenic area has introduced night tour programs for tourists since April 10. (Xinhua/Li Jia'nan)
A villager works on a field in Zhaxizom Township in Tingri County, southwest China's Tibet Autonomous Region, April 25, 2020. (Xinhua/Sun Fei)
Aerial photo taken on April 29, 2020 shows buffalos crossing the Jialing River in Peng'an County, southwest China's Sichuan Province. (Xinhua/Xue Chen)
Farmers walk in the terraced fields in Yuanyang County of Honghe Hani and Yi Autonomous Prefecture, southwest China's Yunnan Province, April 29, 2020. (Photo by Liang Zhiqiang/Xinhua)
