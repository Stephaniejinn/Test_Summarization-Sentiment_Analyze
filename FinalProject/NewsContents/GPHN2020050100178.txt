Two more lab-confirmed COVID-19 cases and fourth death reported by Public Health
SHARE ON:
The COVID-19 virus (Photo credit: supplied)
Hastings-Prince Edward Public Health now reports 41 cases of COVID-19 in the two Counties.
Two more cases have been added since Wednesday, and one more death is being reported as well bringing the total to four. 20 people have gone on to recover after contracting the virus.
Public Health reports there are 215 probable cases of the virus.
2,544 tests have been done by Public Health as of Thursday morning, with 2,253 coming back negative. That means the positivity rate for testing sits at 1.8-percent.
The amount of lab-confirmed cases in North Hastings continues to sit between five and nine, according to Public Health.
