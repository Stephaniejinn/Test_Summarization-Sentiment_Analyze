Title : Global COVID-19 deaths surpass 250,000 -- Johns Hopkins University
Ariticle : 


The United States reported the most COVID-19 deaths at 68,387.
NEW YORK, May 4 (Xinhua) -- Global COVID-19 deaths surpassed 250,000 on Monday afternoon, reaching 250,134 as of 4:40 p.m. (2040 GMT), according to the Center for Systems Science and Engineering at Johns Hopkins University.
A total of 3,571,615 cases have been reported from over 180 countries and regions in the world, according to the CSSE.
People have fun at Washington Square Park in New York, the United States, as temperature rises on May 3, 2020. (Photo by Michael Nagle/Xinhua)
The United States reported the most COVID-19 deaths at 68,387. Other countries with over 20,000 fatalities included Spain, Italy, Britain and France, the CSSE data showed.
