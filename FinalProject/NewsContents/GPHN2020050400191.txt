Title : Confirmed Coronavirus Cases Reach 2,558 in Nigeria, Health Agency Says
Ariticle : 
Special to Dow Jones Newswires
IBADAN, Nigeria--The number of confirmed cases of Covid-19 in Nigeria has risen to 2,558, up from 2,170 on Friday, the Nigeria Center for Disease Control said Sunday evening.
According to the NCDC, there have now been 87 coronavirus-related deaths in the West African country, up from 68 on Friday, while 400 patients have been discharged.
Lagos state, the largest commercial center in Nigeria, has 1,107 confirmed cases, the highest in the country, followed by 342 in Kano, northern Nigeria's commercial hub, and 278 in the federal capital territory of Abuja, the NCDC said.
The health agency said the coronavirus is in 35 of the country's 36 states, all but Cross River state in Nigeria's southeast region.
