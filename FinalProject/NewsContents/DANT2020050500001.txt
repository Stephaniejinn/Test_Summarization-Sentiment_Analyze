Title : Covid-19 patient in Henan died from cirrhosis
Ariticle : 

A 64-year-old man, Binh Luc, Ha Nam died early in the morning.

Previously, the patient was treated in the Department of Gastroenterology of Ha Nam General Hospital from March 20 with end-stage cirrhosis, exhaustion, severe gout, stiffness, muscle atrophy.

On April 7, the patient was confirmed positive for SARS-CoV-2, transferred to Central Hospital for Tropical Diseases for Covid-19 treatment.

After the treatment, the patient had 4 negative tests with SARS-CoV-2 on 10, 12, 15 and 17/4, the patient was determined to recover from the Central Hospital of Tropical Diseases, Covid-19 is no longer infected, other conditions are stable, referral to treatment of cirrhosis.

From April 17, the patient was treated for cirrhosis at the provincial level, no longer infected with the SARS-CoV-2 virus.

However, due to very severe end-stage cirrhosis, before death the patient presents with coma due to severe hepatic dysfunction and electrolyte disorders. The patient died with a diagnosis of end-stage cirrhosis.

When the patient died, Ha Nam General Hospital conducted a SARS-CoV-2 test that turned negative, the patient did not have any symptoms of lung damage caused by Covid-19.

Concerning this death case, on 4/5/2020, the Ministry of Health Professional Council met with leading experts and determined that this death was not caused by Covid-19 but by cirrhotic patients. final phase.

So far, Vietnam recorded 271 cases of Covid-19, no deaths have been reported.
