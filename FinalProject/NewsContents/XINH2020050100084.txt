Title : Chimelong Safari Park in Guangzhou reopens
Ariticle : 
A panda-shaped handmade toy (R) made by a fan is seen at the Chimelong Safari Park in Guangzhou, capital of south China's Guangdong Province, April 30, 2020.(Xinhua/Liu Dawei) 
GUANGZHOU, May 1 (Xinhua) -- The Chimelong Safari Park reopened to the public on Thursday with strict coronavirus pandemic prevention measures.
