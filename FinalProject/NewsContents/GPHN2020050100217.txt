Title : COVID-19: Oyo Records Two New Cases, As Total Cases Rise To 23
Ariticle : 
IBADAN– Nigeria Centre For Disease Control (NCDC), has announced that Oyo state has recorded another new two cases of Covid-19.
With these new two cases, it has brought the total number of confirmed cases in the state to twenty-three (23) which is the thirteenth highest in the country.
However, the country has recorded 204 new cases, which is the highest since the outbreak of the pandemic virus.
