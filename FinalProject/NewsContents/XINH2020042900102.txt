Title : Chinese medical experts support Burkina Faso in fight against COVID-19
Ariticle : 
 OUAGADOUGOU, April 29 (Xinhua) -- Twelve Chinese medical experts, who arrived in Burkina Faso's capital Ouagadougou on April 16 to help the west African country in the fight against COVID-19, have had a busy schedule in the last two weeks.
They shared their expertise and provided technical and practical advice so as to improve the country's capacity to control COVID-19.
