Title : COVID-19 cases in Sri Lanka pass 700 mark
Ariticle : 
The coronavirus cases reported from Sri Lanka surpassed the 700th mark with new cases identified today (02), stated the Ministry of Health.
Twelve fresh cases of COVID-19 infections have been since 6.15 pm this evening.
This brings the total number of novel coronavirus infections in the country to 702.

Seven COVID- 19 patients in the country have succumbed to the virus.
