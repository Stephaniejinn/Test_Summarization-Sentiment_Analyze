Title : German Health Minister: It may take several years to develop a new crown vaccine
Ariticle : 
Original title: Reference News: German Health Minister says it may take &quot;several years&quot; to develop a new crown vaccine
Reference News Network reported on May 4 According to Agence France-Presse Berlin on May 4, the German Minister of Health said that it may take &quot;several years&quot; to develop a new coronavirus vaccine. Previously, US President Trump predicted that this goal could be achieved by the end of 2020.
On the evening of the 3rd local time, Jens Span said on TV: &quot;If I can achieve this goal within a few months, I will be very happy. But the development of the vaccine may also take years, because of course there may be setbacks, Just like what we have seen in other vaccine development processes. &quot;(Compilation / Yang Xinpeng)
