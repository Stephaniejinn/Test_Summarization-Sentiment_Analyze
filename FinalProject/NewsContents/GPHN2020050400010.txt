Title : COVID-19 : Ebonyi Records 5th Case
Ariticle : 
Abakaliki – Efforts of Ebonyi state government to forestal further spread of COVID-19 during the weekend suffered a setback as two more cases of COVID-19 were confirmed in Ebonyi, bringing the total number of cases in the state to five.
These were recorded within two weeks.
Governor Umahi made the disclosure Sarurday night in a telephone interview with the Ebonyi State COVID 19 situation room, while giving update on the pandamic

The Governor explained that the 4th case who is a 22- year old boy who returned from Ojo in Lagos state, had the virus while the fifth case got the virus from the third case at the Pa Oruta Ngele Stadium Abakaliki holding area.
According to the Governor, “I want to announce that we have a 4th and 5th case of COVID-19 in Ebonyi state. The 4th case is a 22- year-old boy who lives in Ojo Lagos State just like the 3rd case. He left Lagos on on the 25th of April and entered Ebonyi through Nkalagu border and from there entered bus to Ezzamgbo junction.”
The Governor noted that “thereafter, he entered bus from Ezzamgbo junction to Effuim where he entered Okada to Ishieke before he was caught by security who intercepted him and brought him to the stadium on the 27th of April.”
He stated that by the directive of government his sample was collected and by Saturday evening, it turned out to be positive.
Governor Umahi pointed out that the patient has been removed from the Isolation center (Stadium) to the treatment center at Unity square Abakaliki.
