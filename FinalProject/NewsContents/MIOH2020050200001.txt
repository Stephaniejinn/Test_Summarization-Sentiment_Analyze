Title : Brazil recorded 91,589 cases of coronavirus and 6,329 deaths from the disease
Ariticle : 
The information was updated by State Health Departments to 14h on Friday (01). Of the 91,589 confirmed cases, 38 039 (41.5) are recovered and other 47 221 (51.6%) were followed
The Ministry of Health reported 91,589 cases of coronavirus and 6,329 deaths from the disease in Brazil until 14h on Friday (01), according to information forwarded by the State Health Departments across the country. In the last 24 hours, there were 6,209 new cases and 428 new deaths. Of the 91,589 confirmed cases, 38,039 are recovered (41.5%) and other 47 221 were in attendance.Currently, all states recorded cases and deaths from coronavirus.
