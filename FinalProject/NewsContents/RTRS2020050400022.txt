Title : Mexico reports 1,383 new coronavirus cases, 93 more deaths
Ariticle : 
MEXICO CITY (Reuters) - Mexico’s health ministry reported 1,383 new coronavirus cases and 93 more deaths on Sunday, bringing the country’s total to 23,471 cases and 2,154 deaths.     
Of Mexico’s 32 federal entities, only two have registered fewer than 100 cases, deputy health minister Hugo Lopez-Gatell told a news conference.
