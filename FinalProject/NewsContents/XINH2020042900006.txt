Title : Just In: Latest from French PM's announcement on coronavirus measures
Ariticle : 
French Civil Protection volunteers disinfect their equipment in Pantin near Paris, France, April 22, 2020.

PARIS, April 28 (Xinhua) -- The following are extracts from French Prime Minister Edouard Philippe's latest announcement on the country's coronavirus pandemic situation.
-- France will not lift lockdown on May 11 if COVID-19 indicators are not as good as expected;
-- Wearing masks compulsory in public transport from May 11;
-- Wearing masks will be compulsory in secondary schools;
-- All shops except cafes and restaurants will reopen from May 11;
-- Gatherings of over 10 people banned after May 11, no events with over 5,000 people allowed till September.
