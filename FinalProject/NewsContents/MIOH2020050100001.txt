Title : Brazil recorded 85,380 cases of coronavirus and 5,901 deaths from the disease
Ariticle : 
The information was updated by State Health Departments to 14h on Thursday (30). Of the 85,380 confirmed cases, 35,935 (42%) are recovered and the other 43,544 are being assisted
The Ministry of Health reported 85,380 cases of coronavirus and 5,901 deaths from the disease in Brazil until 14h on Thursday (30), according to information forwarded by the State Health Departments across the country. In the last 24 hours, there were 7,218 new cases and 435 new deaths. Of the 85,380 confirmed cases, 35,935 are recovered (42%) and other 43 544 were in attendance.
Currently, all states recorded cases and deaths from coronavirus. São Paulo concentrates most of the reports, with 28,698 cases and 2,375 deaths. Rio de Janeiro is in second place, with 9,453 cases and 854 deaths. The state registers fewer notifications is Tocantins, with 137 confirmations and three deaths.
