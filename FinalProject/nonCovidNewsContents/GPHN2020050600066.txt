Title : Covid-19: PAT tests two active ingredients. Covid-19: PAT teste deux principes actifs.
Ariticle : 
On 16 Apr 2020, Plant Advanced Technologies (PAT) initiated preliminary anti-SARS-CoV-2 evaluations on two of its active ingredients in development. These active ingredients would have shown in vivo and in vitro capability to control general inflammation, as well as an in vitro antiviral ability on respiratory viruses of influenza (H1N1) and bronchiolitis type (RSV). PAT did not disclose the active ingredients evaluated in the study. Original Source: Chimie Pharma Hebdo, Website: http://www.industrie.com/chimie/, Copyright ETAI Information 2020.
