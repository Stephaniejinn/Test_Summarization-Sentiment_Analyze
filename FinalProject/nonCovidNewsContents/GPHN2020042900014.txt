Title : Brazil: Covid-19 outbreak infects 20 JBS workers in Passo Fundo
Ariticle : 
Folha de S. Paulo, 26 Apr 2020, Online:- An outbreak of Covid-19 has infected 20 workers of Brazilian meat processing firm JBS, at its factory in Passo Fundo, in the state of Rio Grande do Sul. The unit was shutdown on 24 April 2020, with a source claiming that 12 workers at the factory had already previously been infected with the virus. JBS stated that it is following the guidelines established by local authorities, maintaining rigorous control at its processing factories, distribution centres and offices to avoid further spreading the virus. The city's health board noted that there are currently 78 workers from the factory at home with flu symptoms. The Public Labour Ministry issued a public civil action against JBS, stating that the company did not follow health surveillance guidelines in regards to the crisis. According to investigators, the company refused to sign a deferred prosecution agreement regarding the issue and declined any wrongdoing.
 The company also stated that operations at the factory will be resumed soon.
