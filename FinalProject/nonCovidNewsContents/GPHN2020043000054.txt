Title : Karnataka reports 11 cases
Ariticle : 
It was a mixed bag of results for Bengaluru on Tuesday. While one case was reported from the Padarayanapura cluster, S Suresh Kumar, the Covid-19 spokesperson, said that all the random samples collected from Padarayanapura and Hongasandra have tested negative.
The total number of Covid-19 positive cases in Karnataka was 11 on Tuesday. While an Influenza like Illness (ILI) case was reported on Monday from Padarayana-pura, another cluster contact tested positive on Tuesday.
There has been a consistent increase in the number of cases from this locality where despite the Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike sealing the area, violations continue to be reported every day. Meanwhile, S Suresh Kumar said, "We collected random samples of 145 people from Hongasandra and 70 from Padarayanpura and all of them have tested negative."
Across the state, 14 people were discharged on Tuesday (five in Bengaluru, seven in Mysuru, one in Bengaluru Rural and another patient who is not from the state but was admitted here). Karnataka also ramped up its testing facility where around 4,827 samples were tested, of which 4,717 tested negative, 11 tested positive. The results for the rest are awaited.
