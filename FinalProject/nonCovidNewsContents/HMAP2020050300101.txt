Vesicular stomatitis virus (VSV) was confirmed in one horse on an El Paso County premises. This confirmation marks the 3rd case of VSV in Texas this year [2020].The horse was tested after the owner observed lesions and contacted their veterinary practitioner. The horse has been isolated on the premises and is being monitored.
