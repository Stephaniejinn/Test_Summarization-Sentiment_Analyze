Title : PRO/AH/EDR> Vesicular stomatitis - USA (02): (AZ,TX) horse, mixed st. -
Ariticle : 
In its 22 Apr 2020 Situation Report, the Animal Plant and Health Inspection Service (APHIS) of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) confirmed Arizona's 1st case of vesicular stomatitis virus (VSV) for 2020. As a result, one premises in Cochise County is under quarantine. Cochise is Arizona's southeastern-most county.The index premises houses 2 horses and 5 calves.
