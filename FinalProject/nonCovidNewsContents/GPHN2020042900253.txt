Title : Second Covid-19 wave expected in the fall - Vinkele
Ariticle : 
RIGA, Apr 29, BNS - Latvian experts and specialists of the World Health Organization forecast that the second wave of Covid-19 is expected in the autumn of this year, at the same time as the flu season, Minister of Health Ilze Vinkele (For Development) said at a meeting of the Saeima Sustainable Development Committee on Wednesday.
"Summer has been given to us to prepare so that in the autumn we will be adapted to living with Covid-19 for a longer period of time," said Vinkele.
According to the minister, specialists are concerned about the fact that the flu season will also begin in the autumn, which would mean that the spread of two infectious diseases will have to be limited.
Dace Murmane-Umbrasko, State Secretary at the Ministry of Health, added that in such circumstances it would be necessary to strengthen the capacity of the Center for Disease Prevention and Control (SPKC) and create a mobile application that would allow more efficient contact searches of patients. Among other things, it is necessary to improve the e-health system to ensure remote and efficient exchange of information.
According to her, it is equally important to ensure a social security network strategy for low-income people, including the provision of basic health care services and access to basic medicines.
